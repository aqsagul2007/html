Choclate Cake

Ingredients

  • 1.5 Cup sweet choclate powder.
  • 2.5 Cups flour.
  • 2 Teaspoons of baking powder.
  • 1.5 Cups of sugar.
  • 3 large eggs.
  • 1.5 Cups of butter.
  • 1.5 Cup of half and half[half milk half cream in packets:if not use normal milk and cream salt to taste.

Method

  1. Mix choclate powder,flour and powder together and sieve into a big bowl.
  2. Beat the butter and sugar well into a cream.
  3. Add eggs one by one into it and keep on beet.
  4. Gradually aad the dry flour mix into the butter mixture and while adding keep on beating.
  5. At the same time add the half and half to keep the mixture moist.
  6. Once everything ,is added and the salt and mix well.
  7. Bake in a greased baking try for 20 minutes in 150 gas mark and poke a stick to check if it is done well. if nothing sticky comes out with the stick the cake is done.
  8. Turn the cake onto a wire rack and let it cool.
  9. You can either serve this with cream beaten with icing sugar or covered with normal choclate butter icing.
