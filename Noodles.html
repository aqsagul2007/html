Ingredients

  • 3Cup of Water.
  • Noodles.
  • Noodles Masala.
  • Chat Masala.
  • Lemon.

Method

  1. Sab se pehle water boil krnay k liye rakh den.
  2. Water boil honay k bad noodles,Noodles masala,Chat masala or lemon water mein dal den.
  3. Noodles gal janay tak pakaayen.
  4. Garam garam mehmaano k saamnay pesh kren.
