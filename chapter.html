Scrambled Eggs

Eggs are one of my foods. Here is a recipe for deliciously rich scrambled eggs.

Ingredients

  • 2eggs
  • 1tib butter
  • 2tibs cream

Method

  1. Melt butter in a frying pan over a medium heat
  2. Gently mix the eggs and cream in a bowl
  3. Once butter has melted at cream
  4. Using a spatula fold the eggs from the edge of the pan to the centre every 20 seconds(as if you are making an omelette)
  5. When the eggs are still moist remove from the heat(it will continue to cook on the plate untill served)
